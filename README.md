## ğŸ§  Scientific Context

Cells constantly exchange materials such as oxygen, carbon dioxide, ions, and water through selectively permeable membranes.  
This poster highlights:
- **Diffusion:** Passive movement of molecules down a gradient  
- **Osmosis:** Diffusion of water across a partially permeable membrane  
- **Active Transport:** ATP-powered movement against concentration gradients  

Together, these processes maintain **homeostasis** and support life in all organisms.
